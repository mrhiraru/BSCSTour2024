DAY 1: PAGASA WEATHER STATION

On Day 1, the first company we visited was the PAGASA Weather Station at Subic Bay International Airport. We learned about weather forecasting and how technology helps us understand weather patterns. The station showed us how meteorology helps aviation. We saw the tools used to watch the weather and make predictions. These tools are important for keeping flights safe and airports running smoothly.

DAY 1: FIRST AVIATION ACADEMY

The second company we visited was the First Aviation Academy, still at Subic Bay. During our visit, we had the chance to see their collection of airplanes and learn about their advanced airplane simulator used by pilot students. The simulator at First Aviation Academy offers students a realistic training environment, helping them develop essential skills for aviation. It was interesting to observe how these simulators replicate real flight conditions, preparing future pilots for various challenges they may encounter in their careers.

DAY 1: PORT OF SUBIC BAY

The third company we visited was the Port of Subic Bay, we gained insights about vessel traffic management system. This system utilizes radar, CCTV, VHF radio, and an automatic identification system to monitor marine traffic and ensure safe navigation. It's the technology they rely on to effectively manage vessel movements within Subic Bay port, demonstrating how advanced technology enhances maritime operations and safety.

DAY 2: QBO INNOVATION HUB

On Day 2, our first stop was at QBO Innovation Hub. QBO is a company dedicated to supporting Filipino startups and fostering their growth. They provide various programs and services tailored to the specific needs of startups, offering essential resources, information, skills, and assistance. Their aim is to create an environment where startups can thrive and succeed in the competitive business landscape.

DAY 2: PACKETWORX INC. IoT TECHNOLOGIES HUB

Our next visit was to PACKETWORX INC. IoT TECHNOLOGIES HUB. They design and develop affordable, wireless, easy-to-install, and energy-efficient devices. These devices, along with their connectivity solutions, help businesses and solution providers set up and oversee IoT solutions easily. During our visit, they showed us their IoT products like flood monitoring and air condition monitoring devices. These technologies benefit daily life by improving efficiency and providing better monitoring, which can contribute to safety and optimize energy use effectively.
