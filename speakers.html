The Speakers

Karen Kayron Love Montenegro

Subic Bay

Ms. Karen was our tour guide at Subic Bay, told us about the attractions and activities there. She explained the places we passed by as we ventured through Subic Bay.

Arnel Gonzalez

PAGASA Weather Station

Mr. Arnel explained how technology helps us understand weather patterns for aviation safety. He showed us tools used to predict weather, essential for safe flights and smooth airport operations.

Dave Del Rosario

First Avaition Academy

Mr. Dave explained what is FAA all about, showed us their airplanes, and talked about the advanced flight simulators they use for training pilots.

Engr. Jerome D. Saddi, ECE

Port of Subic Bay

Engineer Jerome talked about the Vessel Traffic Management System. He explained how the system monitors marine traffic using radar, CCTV, VHF radio, and an automatic identification system to ensure safe navigation and efficient vessel management.

Karla Legaspi

QBO Innovation Hub

Karla Legaspi shared insights about QBO Innovation Hub, highlighting how they support Filipino startups with tailored programs and resources to foster growth and innovation in the business community.

King Jeather Cruz

Packetworx Inc. IoT Technology Hub

Mr. Cruz discussed Packetworx, focusing on their IoT products like flood and air condition monitoring devices. He explained how their technology simplifies IoT solutions for businesses and enhances monitoring capabilities.

Adrian Saldana

Light Rail Transit Authority - Line 2

Mr. Saldana gave us a detailed tour of the Light Rail Transit Authority - Line 2, explaining its operations and facilities, including train maintenance and the control room overseeing operations.

Berna Romulo Puyat

National Museum of the Philippines

Ms. Berna provided us with an insightful discussion on the history and significance of "Spoliarium" by Juan Luna at the National Museum of the Philippines. She highlighted its impact on Filipino art and culture, offering deeper context to the artwork's portrayal of historical themes and social commentary.

Brian Tan Seng

98 Labs Inc.

Mr Brian Tan Seng shared insights about 98Labs, discussing their projects and his journey as the founder. He elaborated on the company's innovations and their impact on the tech industry.

Gretchel Listana

Hytec Power Inc.

Gretchel Listana discussed Hytec's technologies, emphasizing their robots, simulators, and training equipment. She explained their applications and benefits in various industries, highlighting their role in advancing technological capabilities.
